# Dear Soulmate

Dear Soulmate is a small  love themed website made by me using plain HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

There are two glowing souls floating in space. 
Drag them close to each other and they slowly connect, merge into a heart, and reveal a romantic message.
It is simple, peaceful, and honestly looks pretty cool hahaha.


## Features

* Draggable glowing souls.... You can move both souls around the screen.
* Animated background.... Stars and small particles move in the background.
* Connection effect.... A glowing line appears when the souls get close.
* Heart animation ...The souls turn into a heart when they meet.
* Final message .. A romantic message appears after the animation.
* Love letter popup...  You can open and read a small love letter.
* Replay button... – You can restart the animation anytime.
* Mouse and touch support...  It works on both computers and mobile phones.
* Phone tilt effect.... The animation moves when you tilt a supported phone.


## How To Use

* Open the demo link
* Drag one soul toward the other soul
* Bring them close enough to make them connect
* Wait for both souls to merge into a heart
* Click the letter button to read the hidden message
* Click the replay button to start again

On mobile, you can use two fingers to pull the souls apart after they merge.

On desktop, right click the merged heart to separate them.

## Tech Stack

* HTML
* CSS
* JavaScript

## Why I Made It

I wanted to make something simple and beautiful that feels like two people finding each other in a huge universe.

It is not really a game. It is more like a small interactive love message made with a lots of heart.
